Board 1 (Master):
LCD16x2:

RS → GPIO12
EN → GPIO14
D4 → GPIO27
D5 → GPIO26
D6 → GPIO25
D7 → GPIO33
VO → Potentiometer (contrast control)
VSS → GND
VDD → 5V
A → 5V (backlight)
K → GND (backlight)
Joystick:

VCC → 3.3V
GND → GND
VRX → GPIO34
VRY → GPIO35
SW → GPIO32
Buttons for Game Mode:

Button 1 → GPIO13 (Single Player) - D23
Button 2 → GPIO12 (Multiplayer) - D22
Board 2 (Slave):
Buttons for Player 2 (Up and Down):
Button 1 (Up) → GPIO14
Button 2 (Down) → GPIO15
Both buttons' other pin → GND
